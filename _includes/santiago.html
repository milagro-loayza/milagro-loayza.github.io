#GoodNews | Dogs rescued by the Canine Brigade will now be able to live in buildings made from recycled plastic eco-blocks. 🐕♻️ (1/3)

What parks can you visit at Easter? 🤗🌳
✅ Parque de la Amistad.
🗓️ From 9 a.m. to 9 p.m., throughout the holiday.
📌 Remember, there are no visitors on Mondays.

✅ Voices for Climate Park.
🗓️ From 10 a.m. to 7 p.m., throughout the holiday.
📌 To enter you must bring two plastic bottles (per person).

✅ Parque del Aire. 🗓️ From 10 a.m. to 7 p.m., during the whole holiday.
📌 Admission is by bringing any recyclable material.
Enjoy these 3 places as a family and create nice memories together. 👨👩👧👦
