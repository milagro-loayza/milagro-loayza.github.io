Mini-plant run by women! 🙋

A decade ago, women leaders of the Kichwa Native Communities, in the San Martin region, decided to join forces and, together with their families, industrialize the production of goods with medicinal qualities. 🇵🇪
Today we see the goal accomplished thanks to the Business Plan co-financed by Agroideas, which made it possible to improve with agricultural technology the processing of cat's claw. 🍃
✅ This is the story of how they managed to realize their goals. 💪

The history of the Agrarian Cooperative of Organic Producers Apromalpi is synonymous with perseverance. 💪😊
The agrarian organization located in Piura and made up of 118 families, accessed a Business Plan that allowed the implementation of a frozen mango processing plant, which will increase the production and marketing of its value-added mangoes, which will be exported to countries such as Holland, Germany, Canada, among others 👨👩👧👦🥭🌍🥭🥭🌍.
With the presence of the Executive Director of Agroideas Carlos Anderson Del Val, this infrastructure was launched with an investment of more than S/ 2.2 million. We continue working for family agriculture! 🇵🇪🌱

Campaign Spotlight: If you want to be strong, you have to fuel up

This spotlight is part of the campaign 'If you want to be strong, you have to fuel up', seeking to promote the consumption of Peruvian potatoes and revalue the work of farmers in the countryside.

There are more than 700 thousand families dedicated to the cultivation of potatoes, a wonderful tuber, companion and protagonist in our cuisine and a must today on our tables, for its nutritional contribution. Let's value and recognize their hard work by consuming Peruvian potatoes because to be strong, #HayQuePapear!
